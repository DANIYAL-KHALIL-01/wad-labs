ACADEMICS

Department of Computer Sciences

The department offers PhD, MPhil, MS (Information Science & Technology), M.Sc. and BS degrees. Each degree programme has a specific objective and focus. The course of study and syllabi for each degree is updated and in line with its objectives.

PhD

PhD is the highest degree awarded in an academic discipline and is the most satisfying and rewarding educational experience. Students work closely with a faculty member, performing original research, and tackling challenging and unsolved problems.

(More details..)

M.Phil Computer Science

The objective of the M.Phil Computer Science programme is to train students to initiate research work. The duration of the M.Phil programme is two years (4 semesters). It consists of 50 credit hours: 24 credit hours for course work and 26 credit hours for research.

(More details..)

MS (Information Science & Technology)

The main aim of the MS programme in IST is to design and develop information-related applications using IT, understanding the relationship between digital information, people, and technology. Offered part-time (6 credit hours per semester) for students with relevant experience and prior degrees.

(More details..)

MS (Data Science)

This program prepares students to analyze and understand vast data generated by various sources. It covers programming, data structures, visualization, and big data analytics, preparing students for both industry and PhD studies.

(More details..)

BS Computer Science

The BS program provides a balance of theoretical and practical knowledge for developing reliable software tools. Students can specialize in one of the following focus areas in the final three semesters:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Software Engineering
  • Data Science
  • Human Centered Computing
  • Platform Based Development
  • Networks and Security
  • Information Management
